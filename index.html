Biography

I will join Show Lab @ NUS as a Ph.D. student in Aug. 2022, working with Prof. Mike Zheng Shou. Before that, I spend a wonderful year at Tencent as an intern, working with Dr. Wei Liu. I received my M.Sc degree in Computer Science at Shenzhen University in 2022, where I received my B.Sc. degree in Physics in 2019.

My research interests lie in Vision-Language Understanding and Egocentric Visual Perception.
