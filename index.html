Biography

Hi there! I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Show Lab @ NUS, working with Prof. Mike Shou. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc and M.Sc degree in Shenzhen University.

My research interests lie in Vision and Language, especially Long-form Video Understanding and Large Language Models.
